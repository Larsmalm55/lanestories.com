A taste of the story

This is one stop on the Bergen route. There are 4 such stories — each with a hidden tip only you will know.

"A Dutch merchant lived in this house for over twenty years — yet according to the church records of Bergen Cathedral, he did not exist. When they finally opened the cellar, they found 47 sealed letters addressed to the Dutch Embassy in Copenhagen. No one knows what they contained."

— Hollendergaten 3, Bergen · 1641

Every stop has a story like this — and one secret tip unavailable anywhere else.
