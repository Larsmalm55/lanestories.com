🕯️ The Dark Lanes · Bergen · Bergenhus Fortress

Stop 1 of 4

You are standing at the heart of Bergen's entire history. This spot — once called Holmen — has been continuously occupied since before the year 1000. Viking kings held court here. Norway's first capital was here. And on the morning of 9 April 1940, it fell in darkness. Before all of that, this was a place of extraordinary ceremony. King Håkon Håkonsson built the great stone hall to your left between 1247 and 1261. It was here, on 11 September 1261, that his son Magnus Lagabøte was crowned...
