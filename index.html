Bergenhus Festning

You are standing at the heart of Bergen's entire history. This spot — once called Holmen — has been continuously occupied since before the year 1000. Viking kings held court here. Norway's first capital was here. And on the morning of 9 April 1940, it fell in darkness. Before all of that, this was a place of extraordinary ceremony. King Håkon Håkonsson built the great stone hall to your left between 1247 and 1261...
